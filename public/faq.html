How To Play
Drafting a Roster

All Spotify artists who are within the monthly listener limits for the league you are joining are fair game. You could select artists you know are coming out with music soon or you can select artists who you just love and believe in, there's no one strategy. You can connect with Spotify to get recommendations who fit the listener limits for the league you are joining.

Scoring

Your overall score is an average of the scores of every artist on your team active or inactive. Each artist score is calculated as the % change in their Spotify monthly listeners since they were added to your team. For example, if you draft or add an artist and over the course of 2 weeks they go from 100k monthly listeners to 110k monthly listeners your base score is 10% (10pts). There is, however, a loyalty bonus to incentivize you to stick with your artists long-term where you get an additional 5% boost to your artist score for every week the artist is on your team. So for our example artist who has been on our team for 2 weeks, the base score is 10% (10 pts), but with the loyalty bonus it's 11% (11pts) 10 x (1 + .05*2). Having an artist on your team is one thing, but a true loyal fan is bumping their songs day in and day out, so if you connect your Spotify account and any of your artists breach your top 5 that's an additional 40% boost to that artist's score. Sticking with our example artist from above that would be 15.4 pts (11 * 1.4).

Adding/Dropping

You may add and drop artists from your team at any time on your roster page, but the score they accumulated while on your team is a part of your score for the whole season, so adding/dropping too many artists if they didn't accumulate a lot of listeners while on your team can dilute your overall score and make it harder to win. Also, you are rewarded for loyalty to your artists. If you drop an artist, you may not re-add them at a later date, the drop is permanent. Additionally, you can just click an artist on your team to add them to the drop slot instead of having to search their name.

FAQ
Q: I got a weird message/error when I tried to do something completely normal, have you ever made a website before?

A: No so there will for sure be bugs, please do let me know if you encounter them at eahochberger@gmail.com and I'll help you troubleshoot. I'll also be doing my best to proactively improve the site along the way.

Q: My team is boring, can I see what other teams having going on in more detail?

A: Yes, all team names on the standings are clickable and lead you to a team view page :)

Q: Why is it called "Play&R?"

A: The department at record labels that finds new artists is called "Artists & Repertoire" which is commonly abbreviated as "A&R."
