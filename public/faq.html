FAQ
Q: How are scores calculated again? You explained it to me once but it sounded kinda math-y and I zoned out.

A: Scores are calculated based on the average % difference in Monthly Listeners from the most recent calculation of the monthly listeners to their "baseline" value which was established when they were drafted. So for example if when you drafted your artist they had 100k Monthly Listeners and then that increased to 110k Monthly Listeners they would earn your team 10 points. Additionally, you are rewarded for loyalty to your artists - your artist's score gets a 5% additional bonus for every week the artist stays on your team.

Q: My team sucks. Can I change who is on it?

A: Yes - You can find the add/drop functionality at the bottom of the Roster page and those add/drops process in real time. All points the artist accrued (or lost) while on your team remain as part of your score calculation after they are dropped.

Q: I got a weird message/error when I tried to do something completely normal, have you ever made a website before?

A: No so there will for sure be bugs, please do let me know if you encounter them at eahochberger@gmail.com and I'll help you troubleshoot. I'll also be doing my best to proactively improve the site along the way.

Q: My team is boring, can I see what other teams having going on in more detail?

A: Yes, all team names on the standings are clickable and lead you to a team view page :)
